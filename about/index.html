Ezra Caltum is an information security practitioner, with a passion for reverse engineering, data analysis, and exploitation. He is the leader of the Tel Aviv DC9723 Defcon group and a co-founder and organizer of BSidesTLV.

Currently, he is working as a Security Research Manager at a Fortune 500 company. Ezra has presented at T2 Infosec Conference, BSidesLV, 44CON, Skytalks, BlackHat Arsenal, AppsecIL.

He is active on twitter as @aCaltum
